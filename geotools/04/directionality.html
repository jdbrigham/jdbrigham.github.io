Sun Direction Planner

Enter a compass direction and tolerance. The tool tells you whether that heading is generally valid for sunrise, sunset, or neither at your location. If valid, it returns recurring month/day ranges with the midpoint date & azimuth (year‑agnostic), and exact upcoming dates in the next 12 months (local time).

Inputs
0°=North, 90°=East, 180°=South, 270°=West
Matches if rise/set azimuth is within ±tolerance.
Default: Boston, MA
West longitudes are negative.
Verdict:
Sunrise range    Sunset range    Target direction
Notes:
  • Azimuth convention: degrees from North, increasing clockwise (0°=N, 90°=E, 180°=S, 270°=W).
  • Rise/set elevation uses −0.833° to account for refraction and the Sun’s apparent radius.
  • Recurring month/day ranges are year-agnostic. “Upcoming exact dates” scans from today for 12 months and lists precise local times that match your heading ± tolerance.
