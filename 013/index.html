What is Postcard+?

Postcard+ is a new type of postcard. Each printed card includes a unique QR code on the back. When you scan it with a smartphone camera you'll be able to view a slow motion video of the scene from which the photo was captured. See below for more detailed instructions.

1 - Locate the code

Look on the back of your printed Postcard+ card for the QR code in the upper left corner.

2 - Scan the code

Use the camera or a QR code scanning app on your smartphone to scan the code.

3 - Watch the video

Use onscreen controls to enjoy the video.
